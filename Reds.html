Reds

Summary

Reds is a soccer team formed in 2012 by Redland School Alumni from the 2002 and 2004 promotions in Santiago, Chile. It started as a small project of former rugby players that were looking to switch sports. These players started participating in the “El Castillo” League in 2009 under the name of “Centella”. After two successful years, Centella qualified to “Liga de Campeones”, which is a league played by current champions of different amateur soccer leagues. Then, looking for greater challenges, Centella entered the prestigious “San Jose” League. There was a division in the team: Some of the players wanted to keep rising the challenges, while others just wanted to have fun without getting to competitive. This division teared Centella apart, the two conflicting groups went different was. The group of players who wanted to get to a more competitive level formed Reds in 2009 and joined the Alumni League of Redland School. The rest is history.

Leagues and Tournaments played by Reds

  • Copa Old Redlanders
  • "El Castillo" League
  • "San Jose" League
  • "Liga de Campeones"

Achievements

"El Castillo" League champions 2009 and 2010
"Old Redlanders" League champions 2013, 2014 and 2015
